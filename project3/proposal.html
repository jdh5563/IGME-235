Gear Shifter Project Proposal

High Concept

Gear Shifter is a racing game where the user will race against an AI around a racetrack trying to be the first one across the finish line.

Genre

Gear Shifter is a Racing game.

Platform

Gear Shifter is designed to be for desktop only, but could have mobile functionality added later down the line.

Story

The story is the user plays as Gil Thunder, an up and coming racer who has made it to the Grand Prix, and must defeat their rival, Chad Lightning in a race for all the glory.

Aesthetics

  • Graphics

    I am going for a more retro art style. I want the graphics to be reminiscent of 8-bit games but with a modern flare.

  • Sound

    I want to have a high octane looped background racing track, custom made car sounds, and small dings for checkpoints like a zone or the finish line.

Gameplay

  • Mechanics

    The player can drive around the track attempting to pass the finish line before their opponent can. They are also in a race against themselves as they try to beat their record time in the race.

  • Controls

    On desktop, the user will use WASD to drive the car and can press `Shift` to boost. For a mobile version, the user may drive forward automatically. They can use a left/right touch control on the left side of the screen to turn and a boost touch control on the right side to boost.

  • Teaching the Game

    There will either be an instructions page where the user can see the controls for the game or they will be taught in a tutorial that teaches them how to complete a lap on the track.

  • Player Learning

    The player must learn how to drive their car, how to optimize boost usage, how to take turns optimally, and if there are any possible shortcuts within the tracks to defeat a racer who is faster than them.
