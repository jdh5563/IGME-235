About Me

Projects

Asteroids

This is a recreation of the classic arcade game Asteroids. In this game, you play as a spaceship attempting to navigate through endless clusters of asteroids, blasting any that get in your way as you rack up points with each successful hit. The goal of the game is to score as many points as possible before running out of lives. In the event that all lives are lost, the game ends and your highscore is saved. The theme of my version of the game is designed to replicate the retro feeling of the basic vector graphics used in the game when it was originally created.
