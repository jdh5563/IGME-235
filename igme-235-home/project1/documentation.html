Design Process

  • Portfolio Goal

    My current goal with the portfolio is to display the projects I'm most proud of so potential employers can see the applications of my skills and so friends and family can see what I have accomplished over the years.

  • Audience

    The audience for my portfolio is anyone interested in my professional work. This can be employers, family, friends, mentors, etc.

  • Things I Want To Include

    I would like to include a list of projects that represent my overall programming skills, references to other sites (GitHub to see all of my projects, LinkedIn to see work experience and skills), a link to my resume for an organized list of what I can do, and contact information so people can reach out to me.

  • How Is My Content Organized?

    I have large text at the top of the page displaying my name with a creative tagline to capture the reader's attention, followed by a portrait of myself. In the main body of content, I start with contact information and a section of text describing myself and what I like to do. Then, I have a list of my projects, with an image/video on the left and a brief description on the right with a link attached to the name that will take the user either to a page with more details or a playable demo. Finally, I have an image of my resume with links to my GitHub and LinkedIn next to it. There is also a link to the resume if the user would like to download it.
